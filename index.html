The Bar

No gimmicks.
Just a good bar.

The Peek Inn is what every neighborhood deserves — a proper corner bar where you know the bartender's name, the Guinness is always cold, and nobody's going to judge you for ordering a hot dog at midnight.

We're at 38 Driggs Ave in Greenpoint. Come in after work. Come in on a Sunday. Bring your friends, bring a book, bring nothing at all. We'll have a stool for you.
